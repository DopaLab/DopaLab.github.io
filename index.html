BloopCam

BloopCam is a Chrome extension that sends a local privacy screen to websites that ask for your camera. Free users keep a readable BloopCam watermark. Pro users can remove it and upload an image or GIF as their privacy screen.

Protect-all-sites on by default

Readable BloopCam watermark
Pro custom image/GIF feed
Local settings
